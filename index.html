About Me

Matyáš Klubal

Hi, I’m Matyáš Klubal — a 3D artist focused on creating detailed scenes, hard-surface designs, and procedural environments. I’ve been working with Blender for several years and enjoy turning ideas into visual stories through lighting, composition, and atmosphere.

My Projects

Railgun Spider

A fully rigged cyber-mechanical spider inspired by my obsession with railguns and futuristic creature design. I created a short animation to showcase its movement and personality.

Project HOPE

A 3-5 minute animated short film created as a personal and school project. The goal was to prove that a single creator can produce a complete short film — and hopefully inspire others to try the same.

Deluxo Animation

A complete recreation of the DeLorean DMC-12 known as the Deluxo from GTA V or the iconic Back to the Future car. I modeled the entire vehicle from scratch and brought it to life in a stylized animation.
